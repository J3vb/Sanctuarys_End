👥 Multiplayer — Co-op

✕

Adventure alongside friends in the same world — you'll see each other move, fight, and can chat. Enemies and loot stay local to each player.

To host: run the included server.js on one machine — npm i ws then node server.js. Everyone (host included) enters that machine's IP and port below. For internet play, forward the port (default 8787) on the host's router. Open the game from a file or http page (not https) so ws:// is allowed.

⚔️ How to Play

✕

Movement & Combat

Left-clickMove, or attack the enemy under the cursor — hold to keep moving/attacking
Right-clickCast your selected skill toward the cursor — hold to keep casting (auto-repeats off cooldown)
1 – 9, 0Pick which skill is active on the bar
SpaceDrink a health potion
QDrink a mana potion
ScrollZoom the camera in and out

World

EInteract — portals, the merchant, the stash, stairs down
TReturn to town instantly
GOpen the waypoint map to fast-travel between towns & areas

Panels

IInventory — click a backpack item to equip, hover to compare
KSkill Forest — drag to pan, scroll to zoom, click a node to allocate
MToggle sound
EscClose any open panel

Tips

Spend points in the forest to grow your hero — small nodes give stats, big Notables and Keystones reshape your build. Dive deeper into the dungeon for better loot; every 5th floor holds a boss. Progress saves automatically.
